the algorithm balances the tree in a different way, 
- the REBALANCE_1 rebalance the whole tree for every insertion 
    (rebalanceing cost O(n)), that is the reason why this algorithm 
    runs the slowest compared to the other when inserting but this 
    guarantees that the tree will always be balanced 
    and searching will be faster
    
- the REBALANCE_100 rebalance every 100 insertion, 
  so the height will tend to decrease after 100 insertion.

- the REBALANCE_1000 same with the other rebalance, 
  except after 1000 insertion, has a really large height before 
  inserting 1000 node as it is not balanced yet,
  that is the reason why it is a bit slow 
  when searching if it has not reached 1000 nodes

- the RANDOM inserts node at different level of the tree with 
  probability of 1/N, that is why the height keeps changing due to the 
  randomness of insertion, with this inserton strategy, 
  we can avoid worst case scenario and get O(log N) behaviour in insertion.
  
- the SPLAY tree, its easy to search for recently inserted nodes 
  because they are kept near the roots  
  (this is why the height is really tall for degenerate tree and the search are fast)
  , and average amortisasion cost when inserting is O(log N) and for worst case scenario its O(N).
  The height also tends to change since nodes would be moved to the root
